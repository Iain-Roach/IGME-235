Gloomhaven

Publishing Info

  • Designed By: Isaac Childres
  • Published By: Cephalofair Games
  • Released: 2017

Description

Gloomhaven is a tactical table-top role-playing game, players take on the role of adventurers with unique abilities and motivations, and cooperate to clear dungeons full of dangerous enemies and traps in order to gain experience and loot. To expand a story fueled by the decisions they make.

Game Categories

  • Genre: Adventure, Exploration, Fantasy, Fighting
  • Players: 1-4
  • Gameplay: Strategic Tabletop RPG
  • 60 - 120 Minute play time
